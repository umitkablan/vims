Search And Replace The Word Under The Cursor
============================================
http://vim.wikia.com/wiki/VimTip464

nnoremap <Leader>s :%s/\<<C-r><C-w>\>/
nnoremap <Leader>s :%s/\<<C-r><C-w>\>/gI<Left><Left><Left>
*> Use cursor position to get the word under it.
    :%s/\k*\%#\k*/my_pattern/
